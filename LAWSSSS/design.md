# AI Legal Assistant - Design Style Guide

## Design Philosophy

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: Deep Navy (#1a2332) - Professional authority and trust
- **Secondary**: Warm Gold (#c9a96e) - Premium legal services
- **Accent**: Soft Sage (#8fa68e) - Calming, supportive environment
- **Background**: Cream White (#faf9f6) - Clean, readable foundation
- **Text**: Charcoal (#2d3748) - High contrast readability

### Typography
- **Display Font**: Tiempos Headline - Bold, authoritative serif for headings
- **Body Font**: Suisse Int'l - Clean, modern sans-serif for content
- **Legal Text**: Source Code Pro - Monospace for law citations and technical content

### Visual Language
- **Professional Authority**: Clean lines, structured layouts, legal document aesthetic
- **Empowering Support**: Warm accent colors, approachable typography
- **Trust & Security**: Consistent spacing, reliable interactions, clear hierarchy
- **Modern Accessibility**: High contrast ratios, responsive design, intuitive navigation

## Visual Effects & Styling

### Used Libraries
- **Anime.js**: Smooth micro-interactions and state transitions
- **Typed.js**: AI response typing effects for chat interface
- **Splitting.js**: Text animation effects for legal headings
- **ECharts.js**: Data visualization for legal statistics
- **p5.js**: Background particle effects representing legal network connections
- **Pixi.js**: Smooth visual effects for chat interface elements

### Animation Effects
- **Typewriter Animation**: Legal responses appear with professional typing effect
- **Fade-in Stagger**: Legal resources and laws appear with elegant timing
- **Hover Transformations**: Interactive elements respond with subtle 3D effects
- **Scroll Parallax**: Background elements move at different speeds for depth

### Header & Navigation Effects
- **Liquid Gradient Flow**: Subtle animated background using CSS gradients
- **Floating Legal Icons**: Scales of justice, gavel, law books with gentle motion
- **Smart Search Animation**: Search bar expands with smooth transitions
- **Breadcrumb Trails**: Navigation path with connecting line animations

### Interactive Elements
- **Chat Message Bubbles**: Smooth appear/disappear with scale and opacity
- **Law Citation Cards**: Flip animations revealing detailed legal information
- **Session Timeline**: Interactive timeline with hover states and progress indicators
- **Download Buttons**: Progress animations for transcript generation

### Background & Atmosphere
- **Legal Network Visualization**: Subtle connecting lines representing legal precedents
- **Paper Texture Overlay**: Subtle legal document texture for authenticity
- **Geometric Patterns**: Professional grid systems and legal document layouts
- **Depth Layers**: Multiple z-index layers creating professional depth

### Responsive Design
- **Mobile-First**: Optimized for women accessing legal help on various devices
- **Touch Interactions**: Large touch targets, swipe gestures for mobile
- **Accessibility**: Screen reader friendly, keyboard navigation support
- **Performance**: Optimized animations that don't compromise functionality

### Professional Legal Aesthetic
- **Document-Inspired**: Clean white spaces, structured information hierarchy
- **Trust Indicators**: Security badges, professional certifications, privacy assurances
- **Legal Symbolism**: Subtle integration of scales, gavels, legal books
- **Authority Colors**: Navy and gold combination conveying legal expertise
